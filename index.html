About Us

Once upon a time, in the mystical year of 1969, our founder Jose P. Rizal (not the national hero, just a guy with great taste in snacks) accidentally tripped over a mango in Antipolo City and decided, “Hey, why not start a store here?”

And so, EAZY-STREET was born. A magical place where convenience meets the street, and where getting what you need is easier than figuring out how to parallel park. Rumor has it, the first sale involved a confused teacher, a dog, and happened during an online class.

Store Location: Antipolo City (the land of hills, views, and very irate drivers)

Store Hours: 7:00 AM - 9:00 PM (Open on weekends, because why not?)
